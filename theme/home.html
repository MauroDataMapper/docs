Mauro Data Mapper

Mauro Data Mapper is an open-source platform that brings clarity and consistency to how data is described, shared, and understood.

You can already see your data, perhaps as rows, columns, and values, but what if you could also see what it means: how it connects, where it comes from, and why it matters?

Mauro gives teams a common language for collaboration and discovery, turning complex information into something clear, shared, and genuinely useful.
